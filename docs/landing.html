  • PPML Estimation: Handles zero trade flows and heteroskedasticity naturally, providing consistent elasticity estimates
  • Fixed Effects: Exporter, importer, and year FE capture multilateral resistance and time trends
  • Gravity Variables: Distance, contiguity, common language, colonial ties, RTAs, GDP, population

Why PPML?

  • Unbiased elasticity estimates (no Jensen's inequality problem)
  • Natural handling of zero trade flows (no arbitrary truncation)
  • Robust to heteroskedasticity in trade data
  • Computationally efficient for large datasets
